Who we are

A bridge between primary care and clinical research sponsors.

Co-MedX is a UK-based, physician-led clinical research start-up dedicated to making high-quality clinical trials accessible through GP and pharmacy networks. We combine hands-on research experience with digital tools to support sponsors and CROs across the full study lifecycle.

What we do

  • Design primary-care-feasible study pathways that integrate smoothly into real-world workflows.
  • Onboard, train and support GPs and community pharmacies as Participant Identification Centres (PICs) or research-ready study sites.
  • Coordinate recruitment, consent and data collection with strong governance and clear documentation standards.
  • Provide transparent performance reporting and insights at both GP and pharmacy level.
  • Operate a state-of-the-art central London clinic for site visits and trial procedures.

Who we work with

Aligned incentives for every stakeholder across the primary-care research pathway.

We support the entire ecosystem surrounding GP- and pharmacy-based research — ensuring that roles, responsibilities and benefits are clearly defined for GPs, pharmacies, sponsors and participants.

Community GPs and pharmacies

New revenue streams, professional development and stronger patient relationships through participation in high-quality research — supported by practical, hands-on guidance from our team.

Sponsors & CROs

Access diverse primary-care populations, accelerate recruitment timelines and generate real-world adherence data by conducting research where patients naturally seek healthcare.

Patients & communities

Safer, more inclusive trials that minimise travel burden and integrate research into familiar care settings, with clear communication and a strong focus on participant experience.

How it works

A simple three-step pathway from expression of interest to active recruitment.

  1. 1

    Expression of interest

    Contact us to join our Primary Care Research Network and explore opportunities to participate in upcoming clinical studies.

  2. 2

    Site onboarding & training

    When a suitable study becomes available, we identify and onboard participating GP practices and pharmacies, provide GCP-aligned training and study materials, and ensure governance, contracting and documentation requirements are in place.

  3. 3

    Recruitment & delivery support

    During active recruitment we support sites with screening, documentation and data capture, while providing sponsors with transparent performance and quality reporting.
