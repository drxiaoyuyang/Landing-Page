Who we are

A bridge between pharmacy networks and sponsors.

Co-MedX is a UK-based clinical research start-up focused on making high-quality clinical trials accessible in everyday pharmacy settings. We combine on-the-ground site experience with digital tools to support sponsors, CROs and community pharmacies across the full study lifecycle.

What we do

  • • Design pharmacy-feasible study pathways that fit into real-world workflows.
  • • Onboard, train and support community pharmacies as research-ready sites.
  • • Coordinate recruitment, consent and data collection with clear governance.
  • • Provide transparent performance reporting and pharmacy-level insights.

Who we work with

Aligned incentives for every stakeholder in the pathway.

Community pharmacies

New revenue streams, professional development and stronger patient relationships through participation in high-quality research – with practical, hands-on support from our team.

Sponsors & CROs

Access diverse primary-care populations, faster recruitment and real-world adherence data by bringing studies into the places where patients already collect medicines and seek advice.

Patients & communities

Safer, more inclusive trials that minimise travel burden and integrate research into routine care, with clear information and a focus on patient experience.

How it works

A simple three-step pathway from concept to recruitment.

  1. 1

    Study design & feasibility

    We review the protocol with you, identify pharmacy-feasible workflows, and map inclusion criteria, data points and safety requirements to real-world settings.

  2. 2

    Pharmacy onboarding & training

    We identify and onboard suitable pharmacies, provide GCP-aligned training and study materials, and ensure governance, contracts and documentation are in place.

  3. 3

    Recruitment & delivery support

    During active recruitment we support pharmacies with screening, documentation and data capture, and provide sponsors with transparent performance and quality reporting.
